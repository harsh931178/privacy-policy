Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to HindustaniPathshala. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile app to access and purchase educational content from Class 9 to 12.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and class details during registration.
  • Payment Information: Collected and processed securely via third-party payment gateways. We do not store your card details.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, course access, and content interaction for analytics and improvement.
  • Device Info: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers for security and support.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide access to purchased courses, videos, and notes.
  • Track learning progress and recommend relevant content.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Send important updates and notifications.
  • Ensure compliance with legal requirements.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your data. We only share your information with:

  • Trusted payment processors for handling transactions.
  • Third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (e.g., Firebase).

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Update or correct your profile information from within the app.
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting our support.
  • Opt-out of non-essential notifications.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
